How It Is Built

Strong & Quality Construction

We use good quality material so your home stays strong for many years. Tap on any item to read more.

Foundation & Structure

+
Built with strong RCC (cement + iron rod) frame. It is earthquake safe and will last for generations.

Walls & Paint

+
Outside walls are 9 inch thick with weather-proof textured paint. Inside walls have good quality Asian or Dulux paint that stays fresh for years.

Flooring

+
Big 48×24 inch vitrified tiles in bedroom, kitchen and living room — looks great and easy to clean. Bathrooms and balcony have anti-slip tiles so no one falls.

Doors & Windows

+
Teak wood door frames with 32 inch wide flush doors. Good quality glass windows that let in light and air and keep noise out.

Kitchen & Bathroom

+
Ready-to-use modular kitchen setup. Bathroom fittings from trusted brands like Kajaria, Kerovit and Hindware — good quality and long lasting.
